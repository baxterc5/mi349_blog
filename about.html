About Chloé

Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Chloé Baxter, a 21-year-old senior at Michigan State University, where I’m studying Information Science with a focus on Human-Centered Interaction and minoring in Business. Raised on a farm in a small town, I always dreamed of exploring the world beyond my hometown. Now, I’m turning that dream into reality, one trip at a time. I love capturing the beauty of new places through storytelling and photography, and I’m excited to share my travel experiences with you.
